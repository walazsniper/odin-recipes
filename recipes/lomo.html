Lomo Saltado Recipe

Description

Lomo Saltado is a Peruvian dish that is inspired from Asian cuisine

It is a staple in pretty much all Peruvian restaurants

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (1)
  • Potatoes (1)
  • Beef (250g)
  • Onions (preferably purple, 1)
  • Soy Sauce (both dark and normal)
  • Salt, pepper
  • Rice
  • Chicken stock
  • Garlic (1)
  • Cooking wine
  • Corn Starch (1 teaspoon)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Defrost the beef the night before
  2. Cut potatoes into fry shapes
  3. Fry potatoes
  4. Remove potatoes
  5. Cut beef into strips or cubes, preferably against the lines
  6. Mince garlic
  7. Season beef with minced garlic, 1/2 cap of cooking wine, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp of corn starch, pepper
  8. Cut onions into strips
  9. Peel and cut tomatoes into strips
  10. Fry beef with olive oil until it turns brown
  11. Remove beef
  12. Fry tomatoes and onions
  13. Add 1/2 cup of water
  14. Add 1 1/2 tbsp of normal soy sauce
  15. Add 1/2 tbsp of dark soy sauce
  16. Add 1/2 tbsp of salt
  17. Add 1/2 tbsp of chicken stock
  18. Stir
  19. Add fries and beef to the mix
  20. Take out of fire and turn off stove
