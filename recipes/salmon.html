Salmon recipe

Description

This salmon recipe follows western cuisine and is very easy to cook!

High in protein, this is an ideal healthy meal

Ingredients

  • Salmon (1 fillet)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic or garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Defrost salmon overnight
  2. Cut through the salmon on the long sides, but don't slice too deep
  3. Season with salt, pepper
  4. Flip and season again
  5. Turn heat on high and set pan
  6. Add olive oil
  7. Put salmon with the skin touching the pan
  8. Use spatula and slightly press down
  9. Flip salmon
  10. Cook each side for 7 min
