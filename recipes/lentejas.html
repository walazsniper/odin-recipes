Lentejas recipe

Description

Lentejas are a popular dish from Chile. It is said that it is inspired from Middle Eastern Cuisine

They're a good source of protein and fiber

Ingredients

  • Lentejas (1/2 cup)
  • Potato (1)
  • Onion (1/4)
  • Carrot (1/4)
  • Salt
  • Chicken Stock
  • Garlic (1 clove)
  • Sibarita spice

Steps

  1. Boil water for lentejas
  2. Mince onion
  3. Mince potato
  4. Grate carrot
  5. Add olive oil to the pot
  6. Fry onion and carrot
  7. Add garlic and sibarita
  8. Stir and add lentejas
  9. Pour hot water in pot, enough to just cover lentejas
  10. Turn heat to high until it starts to boil
  11. Turn heat to low and wait 20 min
  12. Add potatoes, salt, and chicken stock (1 tbsp)
  13. Turn heat to high until it starts to boil
  14. Turn heat to low andwait 10 min
  15. Turn off stove
